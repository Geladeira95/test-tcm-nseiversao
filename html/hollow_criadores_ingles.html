Creators

Team Cherry is an Australian indie game developer formed by three passionate gamers: Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and Nicole E. They gained prominence with the release of Hollow Knight in 2017, a title that quickly became a hallmark of the Metroidvania genre. The story of Team Cherry began with the desire to create a game that captured the essence of classic adventures, blending exploration, challenging combat, and a rich narrative filled with mysteries.

Since its founding, the team has stood out for its meticulous attention to detail in both gameplay and aesthetics. With a hand-drawn art style, Hollow Knight is a visual masterpiece that transports players to the dark and enchanting world of Hallownest. The soundtrack, composed by Christopher Larkin, is also an essential element, providing emotional depth and an immersive atmosphere that complements the game experience.

After the success of Hollow Knight, Team Cherry continued to expand the game’s universe with DLCs that introduced new content and challenges, showcasing their commitment to the player community. The team is known for listening to feedback and engaging with their fan base, reinforcing their reputation as an approachable and dedicated developer. Furthermore, Team Cherry not only focuses on releasing updates but also values the creation of a cohesive and meaningful experience.

With plans for new projects, including a sequel to Hollow Knight titled Silksong, the team promises to continue enchanting players with their creativity and passion for storytelling through games. The anticipation surrounding their releases reflects the trust and affection the gaming community has for Team Cherry, making them a reference point in the indie scene.
