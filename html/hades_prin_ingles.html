Hades is an action roguelike game developed by Supergiant Games, released in 2020. In it, you control Zagreus, the son of Hades, who attempts to escape the Greek underworld in search of his mother, Persephone. The gameplay combines dynamic combat and strategies with a variety of weapons and powers offered by the gods of Olympus.

The design is vibrant, featuring stylized art and well-developed characters, and the narrative unfolds with each escape attempt, revealing new interactions and stories. The engaging soundtrack complements the experience, making Hades both a challenging and captivating game.

Hades stands out for its combination of intense gameplay and rich narrative, providing a challenging and engaging experience. The game blends roguelike elements with frantic combat, where you control Zagreus in his attempt to escape the Greek underworld. Each run features varied enemies and powerful bosses, requiring adaptable combat skills and strategies. As players progress, they unlock new weapons and blessings from the gods, which not only diversify combat tactics but also enrich interactions and the narrative.

The narrative of Hades is presented dynamically, with dialogues and interactions that evolve with each escape attempt. Instead of a linear story, players discover more about the characters and the world through conversations and events, allowing each player to build their own understanding of the plot. This approach, combined with a vibrant artistic style and an engaging soundtrack, makes Hades a memorable and immersive experience.
