Undertale is a 2D RPG developed by Toby Fox, released in 2015. The game stands out for its unique and emotional narrative, combined with innovative gameplay that subverts traditional genre expectations. Set in a world inhabited by monsters, you take on the role of a child who has fallen into this realm. The journey involves decisions that influence the fate of the characters, with a combat system that allows you to either fight or spare enemies, creating multiple outcomes.

The mechanics of Undertale provide a distinct experience where empathy and moral choices play a central role. Its retro aesthetic, inspired by 90s classics, is complemented by engaging dialogue and an appropriate soundtrack, also composed by Toby Fox. Every detail of the game, from the characters to the authentic humor, contributes to creating an atmosphere that oscillates between the comedic and the deeply moving, making it an unforgettable experience.

Undertale is known for its unique approach to difficulty and moral choice. The game offers combat that can be resolved either peacefully or aggressively, requiring strategy and attention to enemy patterns. As the player progresses, new mechanics are introduced, allowing for different approaches and encouraging exploration of the world from new perspectives.

The narrative of Undertale is presented subtly, reinforcing its captivating atmosphere. Instead of a straightforward story, players discover details through enigmatic dialogues and interactions with characters, where their choices affect the unfolding of the plot.
